# Video Streaming and WebRTC Samples

Some samples for video streaming and WebRTC for Unity.

## Demos

* Player: A WebRTC stream player, to pull WebRTC stream from SRS, then render it.
* Publisher: A WebRTC stream publisher, to grab data from WebCamera and Microphone, to push WebRTC stream to SRS.
* Streamer: A WebRTC streamer, to grab data from game camera and voice, to push WebRTC stream to SRS.

You're able to integraty these demos to your application, to work with SRS, a WebRTC SFU server.

Please remove more details at https://github.com/ossrs/srs-unity
